Importance of Maize

Maize is a staple crop that provides food and income for millions of farmers across Kenya.

Farming Tips

  • Plant at the start of the rainy season
  • Use certified seeds
  • Apply organic manure or fertilizer

More Info

Maize requires well-drained soils and regular weeding for optimal growth.
